Documentation

This quick guide explains how to use Cardian's core features for AI-powered car valuation and monitoring.

Manual Lookup

Use Manual Lookup when you already know a car's parameters. Enter details such as brand, model, year, mileage, drivetrain, condition, and other fields. Submit the form to generate a model-based valuation range with AI feedback.

Parsed Lookup

Use Parsed Lookup to evaluate a listing from a URL. Paste a supported listing link, and Cardian will extract available vehicle information automatically, run a valuation pipeline, and return predicted pricing output with contextual interpretation.

Watchlists & Price Alerts

  • Save vehicles to a watchlist directly from lookup results.
  • Track listing changes over time from your Watchlists page.
  • Receive price alerts when Cardian detects movement in tracked listings.

Token System

Cardian uses tokens to power premium actions. Each valuation or advanced workflow consumes tokens based on feature cost. You can top up your balance from the header button or profile area and choose a Starter or Pro package at checkout.

Best Practices

  • Provide complete and accurate car details for better predictions.
  • Compare valuation range with local market conditions before decisions.
  • Use watchlists for vehicles you are actively considering or selling.
